What users told us
Liquidity Misconception · Biggest theme
Users expected DDPI to unlock early exit / premature sell. Most were surprised DDPI is only for demat settlement — not secondary-market liquidity. Expectation gap created significant post-activation disappointment.
Sell Feature Demand · Competitor signal
Strong unmet demand for a secondary-market sell feature. Users mentioned GripInvest allows selling; some were told by Aspero reps that "sell is going live soon" — raising expectations further. Unaddressed, this will drive churn.
Rate Competitiveness · Conversion blocker
A subset did not transact because BondScanner showed a higher YTM (15.20%) for the same bond on Aspero. Rate-sensitive users comparison-shop across platforms; small spread differences are decision-blockers for this cohort.
Browsing / Low Intent
A smaller group was exploring, already understood DDPI correctly, and had no immediate investment need. No conversion friction — just timing.
Liquidity expectation gap — clarify DDPI in-app and prioritize sell feature to address the largest source of confusion and lost conversions
