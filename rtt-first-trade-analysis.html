Key finding: Sunday eSign completers only get RTT 8.2% of the time within 6 hours (vs 84–91% on weekdays). NSDL doesn't process Sunday evening batches. These users receive RTT on Monday morning. Monday then has 2× normal RTT volume (~698 vs ~330–350) but 19.8% trade conversion vs 25.8% on Friday — a 6pp gap. Approximately 13 trades/week are lost because of this Sunday delay.
Fix 1 (Quick win): Add a WebEngage push notification targeting Monday 9–10 AM for users whose KYC_DEMAT_VERIFIED event fired Monday 1–5 AM (overnight NSDL batch). Message: "Your demat account is ready! Start investing." — catch them at the moment of intent.

Fix 2 (Expectation management): On Sunday after eSign success, show an in-app message: "Verification successful! Your demat will be activated Monday morning due to exchange processing hours." This prevents Sunday users from abandoning the app thinking something went wrong.
