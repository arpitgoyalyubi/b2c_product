Why is there an “Unknown” bucket?

Unknown = PAN total − the five persona queries. Users land there when their occupation/income matches no rule (e.g. service/professional under ₹10L, or occupation = Others/Student aged 26–54), when risk or age data is missing (the Jun 22 spike is a likely age-data gap), or as a measurement residual because the five queries are run independently and not yet de-duplicated. A device-tier default fallback + strict priority de-dup would shrink it to a small, stable residual.
