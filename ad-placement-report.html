Channel-by-Channel Verdict
Content / Display
Over-Indexed

92% of impressions, 62% of spend, CPA ₹190. Lowest CPA looks great on paper — but for a bond investment product, Display CTR of 3.35% is 7–15× above India fintech benchmark of 0.2–0.5%.

This signals Google has found a narrow lookalike audience of your existing convertors. It drives first-payment volume but likely misses net-new HNI investors who need trust signals before converting.

Display is the right tool for retargeting (users who installed but didn't complete KYC), not cold prospecting at 23M impressions/week for a niche product.

Search
Under-Indexed

Only 2% of impressions but 27% of spend — and 21% of conversions. Search is the highest-intent channel. A user querying "best corporate bond India 2026" or "FD alternative high return" has already self-qualified.

The declining CTR (7.71% Mar → 3.17% May) as volume grew 7× tells you Google is broadening query matching. Need Search term audit to add negatives for low-intent queries like "money app", "investment app".

For comparable high-consideration Indian fintech (Smallcase, NPS apps), Search is typically 35–50% of spend. We're at 28%.

YouTube
Right Channel, Wrong Scale

YouTube is ideal for bonds — video explains credit ratings, yield mechanics, and builds the trust needed for ₹50K+ investments. India's finance YouTube audience (Pranjal Kamra, CA Rachana Ranade) is exactly your ICP.

Our YouTube CTR is 1.36% — 3× above skippable ad benchmark (0.3–0.5%), which is good. But iOS campaign (`HM_iOS_Install`) using 86% YouTube is the right instinct.

Problem: App campaigns can't target specific YouTube channels. You're getting good CTR but potentially on cricket/news content, not finance channels. Add placement exclusions for entertainment/gaming categories.

Search Partners
Monitor Closely

Clicks spiked ▲80% WoW (699 → 1,259) with only ▲0.7% impression growth. CTR jumped 0.32% → 0.47%.

Search Partners includes Indian portals (Rediff, Indiatimes) where click quality is inconsistent and fraud rates are elevated. At ₹26K spend/week it's small, but the sudden click spike without impression growth is anomalous.

Recommend: reduce Search Partners bid modifier to -30% or exclude entirely and shift budget to Search.
