System-Level Issues

📵 WhatsApp Opt-In Problem — 89–96% Fail Rate Across KYC Journeys

WA fail rates are not journey-specific — they reflect a systemic problem with WA opt-in among KYC-stage users.

Currently WA sends fire for all users regardless of opt-in status. Recommendation: Either (a) gate all WA sends on [WA opt-in = true] user attribute, or (b) add a WA opt-in prompt during the signup/KYC flow to grow the opted-in base. Every failed WA send burns quota and risks WhatsApp blocking the account for spam patterns.

🔔 Push Opt-In Degrades Through the Funnel

Push fail rates increase as users progress deeper into KYC — suggesting users who drop off at later stages are also more likely to have push disabled.
