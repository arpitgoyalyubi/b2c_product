About NTPC Limited

NTPC Limited (National Thermal Power Corporation) is India's largest power generation company and a Maharatna Central Public Sector Enterprise. Founded in 1975, NTPC has a total installed capacity of over 73 GW across thermal, hydro, solar, and wind segments. The company is listed on NSE (NTPC) and BSE (532555), and is a constituent of both Nifty 50 and Sensex.

NTPC bonds are backed by the Government of India's 51%+ equity stake, making them among the safest fixed-income instruments for retail investors. All outstanding NTPC debt is rated CRISIL AAA / ICRA AAA with a Stable outlook.
