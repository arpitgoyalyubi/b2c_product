Attribution limitation: True first-touch, last-touch, time-decay, and U-shaped attribution require per-user event sequences with timestamps — not available via Amplitude's aggregated API. The metrics above are valid approximations: Path Conv% (conditional probability of payment given path usage) and Lift (differential engagement signal). They are not causal attribution. A user counted in 3 touchpoints contributes to all 3 denominators — this is correct and intentional (touchpoints are not mutually exclusive).
