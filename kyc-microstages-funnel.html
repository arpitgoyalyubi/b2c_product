Key Insights last week + this week

⚠️ Biggest in-funnel drop
Of 673 users who opened a UPI app last week, only 576 completed the ₹1 payment — 97 users (14.4%) abandoned inside their UPI app. This is the single largest loss point in the bank funnel. Possible causes: wrong beneficiary shown, app friction, or users testing intent only.
🔧 Engineering: 3.9% pre-screen drop
Exactly 3.9% of users who complete PAN verification never reach the Bank Account View screen — stable across both weeks (34 LW, 14 TW). Root cause: a 3-second success modal + KYC refresh API call must complete before the bank screen renders. Reduce modal to ≤1s or make the refresh non-blocking to recover ~30 users/week.
🔀 7.2% tried both UPI + manual
At least 59 users last week clicked a UPI provider and also entered their account number manually in the same week — meaning their UPI payment failed or expired and they switched to manual. The overlap is higher in weeks where UPI failure rates are elevated (e.g., this week with RPD init errors).
